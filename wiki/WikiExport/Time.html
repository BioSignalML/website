Time

Based on the TimeLine concept -- see http://motools.sf.net/timeline/timeline.html and http://www.ihmc.us/users/phayes/Pub/TimeCatalog.pdf

A Recording has an associated RelativeTimeLine that is a ContinuousTimeLine used to define Instants and Intervals within the recording. The recording may also have a TimeLineMap that determines when its RelativeTimeline starts in terms of some PhysicalTimeLine.

Implementation

We measure time in discrete quanta or "ticks" of 100 nanoseconds (that provides a time span of at least -29,000 to +29,000 years using a signed 64-bit integer to hold the value of a tick). The origin for physical time is 1970-01-01T00:00:00UTC.

References to temporal enities -- Instants and Intervals

The annotation of recordings and signals includes noting events that occur at or over a particular time period, by associating a "time" property to the event.
