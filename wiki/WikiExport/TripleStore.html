Triple Store

Recordings are associated with a triple store by an explicit addRecording() method.

addRecording() associates the Recording with the store -- independently of calling addRecording other statement about the recording and its signals may have been added to the store...

Named graphs are currently not used in the store although each recording's metadata could be held in a separate named graph. I can't see any advantages of doing so -- if anything it would make ad-hoc queries more complex.
