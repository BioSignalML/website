1 Introduction

2. BioSignalML ontology at a glance

An alphabetical index of BioSignalML terms, by class (concepts) and by property (relationships, attributes), are given below. All the terms are hyperlinked to their detailed description for quick reference.

Classes: Annotation, BP_Filter, Dataset, Device, Event, Filter, Format, HP_Filter, LP_Filter, Notch_Filter, Recording, Repository, Resolution, Resource, Signal, Simulation, Source, Transducer, Type, UnitOfMeasure,

Properties: annotation, filter, format, maxFrequency, maxValue, minFrequency, minValue, offset, recording, repository, resolution, sampleRate, scale, signal, signalCount, source, time, type, units,

Instances: BioSignalML, BP, ECG, EDF, EDFplus, EEG, example-notes, FieldML, MFER, RAW, SCP_ECG, SDF, SignalStream, WFDB,
